MYTHICAL MONSTER CARDS

A mythical monster refers to a creature from ancient myths, legends, folklore, or tales that is often imagined as a formidable or supernatural beast.

Chimera

Fire-breathing lion with goat head, snake-tailed monster.

Banshee

Irish spirit wailing foretelling death; otherworldly messenger.

Kraken

Giant sea creature; octopus-like; terrorizes Norse waters.

Wendigo

Cannibalistic spirit; embodies hunger, cold in Algonquian lore.

Nian

Attacks during the Chinese New Year fears loud noises.

Baba Yaga

Witch in mortar, chicken-legged hut, Slavic folklore.

Manticore

Lion body, human face, scorpe tail trumpet voice.

Tengu

Bird-human spirits, protect forests, signal war's approach.

Roc

Immense bird, carries elephants; Middle Eastern legend.

Cerberus

Three-headed dog, guards Greek Underworld's entrance.

La Llorona

Weeps for drowned children, ghostly presence; seeks kids.
